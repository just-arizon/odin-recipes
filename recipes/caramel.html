Caramel Cake Recipe

A very delicious caramel cake with a spread icing cooled cake layers, stack layers, ice top and sides.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of butter
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Make cake: Place white sugar and butter into a mixing bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition
  4. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.
  5. Add vanilla extract
